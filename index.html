About Us

ASME CRCE is an international student chapter of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) network. ASME is a globally recognized organization spanning over 135 countries with 130k+ members. Striving to provide opportunities to mechanical engineering students in their domain, ASME enables students to enhance their knowledge, showcase their skills and increase their professional network. Our chapter is committed to promoting skill development across all engineering disciplines. ASME-CRCE provides students opportunities to demonstrate their talents, learn through professionals and expand their interests through seminars, webinars and various engaging activities within the college.
